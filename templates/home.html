Our Story

About BPIT Cart

Learn about our mission to help college students save money and reduce waste

BPIT Cart was founded by a group of college students who were frustrated with the high cost of college supplies and the waste created when perfectly good items were thrown away at the end of each semester.

Our mission is to create a sustainable marketplace where students can buy and sell used course materials, lab equipment, and other college essentials at affordable prices.

By connecting students directly with each other, we help:

  • Reduce the financial burden of education
  • Promote sustainability through reuse
  • Build a stronger campus community

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of students save money and reduce waste across multiple campuses in India.
